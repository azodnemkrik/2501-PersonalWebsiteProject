This page features a collection of videos I’ve edited, including commercials, fun projects, and creative experiments. Some were made for my kids, while others were just for the enjoyment of bringing ideas to life through editing. Each video is a blend of storytelling, humor, and special effects, making them both entertaining and memorable.

Scrubba Gum-Gum

In this fun and energetic commercial for Scrubba Gum-Gum, my daughter takes center stage to showcase the gum that cleans your teeth while you chew! With a burst of excitement, she explores the many flavors, from classic mint to wild, unexpected twists. Fresh breath, clean teeth, and delicious taste—Scrubba Gum-Gum does it all!

StayCool Insoles

In this action-packed ad for StayCool Insoles, my son puts them to the test, showing how they keep your feet cool and comfortable during any activity. From intense sports to everyday adventures, these insoles provide refreshing relief and soothe aching feet. No matter how hard you play, StayCool Insoles help you stay fresh and ready for more!

Behind the Pixels

This short intro animation for Behind the Pixels sets the stage for our internal meeting on preparing design files for development handoff. With sleek visuals and a dynamic flow, it highlights the key steps in organizing, labeling, and optimizing files for a smooth transition. It’s a quick, engaging way to get everyone ready to dive into the details of efficient design handoff!
